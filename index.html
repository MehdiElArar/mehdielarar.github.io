About Me

I am a third-year PhD student at the Paris-Saclay University, France. My supervisors are Devan Sohier and Pablo de Oliveira Castro. I completed a Master's degree in Applied Mathematics at the University of Lorraine. My research interests include using stochastic tools to perform algorithms under stochastic rounding. I am currently working on providing a tighter probabilistic bounds of linear algebra algorithms.

Research Interests

  • Stochastic rounding, Floating-point arithmetic
  • Numerical linear algebra, Stochastic models of numerical precision
